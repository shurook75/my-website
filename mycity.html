Tafila

Tafila is a small governorate located in the southern part of Jordan. It is known for its beautiful natural landscapes, including mountains, forests, and hot springs. Tafila is rich in history and was once part of the ancient Edomite kingdom. Today, it is a quiet and peaceful area where visitors can enjoy hiking, exploring historical sites, and experiencing authentic Jordanian culture.

Tourist attractions :

Dana nature reserve

Dana nature reserve is the largest nature reserve in Jordan. It covers a variety of landscapes, including mountains, valleys, and desert areas. Dana is home to rich biodiversity, with many rare plants and endangered animals. Visitors can enjoy hiking, birdwatching, and exploring the traditional village of Dana. The reserve plays an important role in protecting Jordan’s natural heritage and promoting eco-tourism.
