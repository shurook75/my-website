Al-Sila castle:

The castle contains palaces, caves, and examples of art, sculpture, and architecture, as well as amazing irrigation systems and watchtowers, at a height of nearly 400 meters above sea level. It covers an area of ​​more than 100 square acres, with a single road, ascending in the form of a winding staircase. The castle is connected by a sloping road from the village of Al-Sila.
